# Naudokis Rules Center

Effective date: June 1, 2026
Version: 2026-06-01
Document ID: **NAUDOKIS-POLICY-HUB-EN-2026-06-01**

This Rules Center explains how the related Naudokis legal documents fit together and which rule to read in a specific situation. It is intended for users who want to quickly find a practical answer before listing an item, reserving an item, making a payment, resolving a dispute, or submitting a privacy request.

## 2. Document Hierarchy

If several documents are inconsistent, they are interpreted in the following order:

1. mandatory provisions of the laws of the Republic of Lithuania and the European Union;
2. terms, price, Deposit, cancellation policy, dates, and handover method clearly shown in the App during the specific Reservation;
3. the special policy for the specific area, for example the Deposit Rules or Cancellation Rules;
4. the Terms of Use;
5. this Rules Center and Help Center explanations.

If mandatory consumer protection applies to a consumer, the documents are interpreted so that mandatory consumer rights are not limited.

## 3. User Journey in One Place

| Stage | What happens | Most important rules |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Listing | The Lessor uploads the item, price, Deposit, condition, availability, handover method, and usage rules | Terms of Use, Prohibited Items Rules, Business Users Rules |
| Reservation request | The Renter selects dates and sees the price, fees, Deposit, and cancellation terms | Terms of Use, Payments Rules, Cancellation Rules |
| Payment | The payment and, where applicable, Deposit are processed through Stripe | Payments Rules, Deposit Rules |
| Confirmation | The Lessor accepts or rejects the request, and the Platform checks readiness, risk, and payment status | Terms of Use, Trust Standard, [Reservation Process Rules](naudokis-reservation-handover-completion-en-2026-06-01.md) |
| Handover | The parties check the item's condition, accessories, instructions, and record evidence | Deposit and Damage Rules, Trust Standard, [Reservation Process Rules](naudokis-reservation-handover-completion-en-2026-06-01.md) |
| Use | The Renter uses the item only as intended, safely, and during the agreed period | Terms of Use, Prohibited Items Rules |
| Return | The item is returned on time, with accessories, and in the agreed condition | Deposit and Damage Rules, [Reservation Process Rules](naudokis-reservation-handover-completion-en-2026-06-01.md) |
| Completion | If there are no issues, the Deposit refund and Lessor payout are initiated | Payments Rules, Deposit Rules, [Reservation Process Rules](naudokis-reservation-handover-completion-en-2026-06-01.md) |
| Dispute | If there is delay, damage, loss, or non-conformity, the parties submit evidence through the Platform | Deposit and Damage Rules, Moderation and Complaint Rules |
| Review | The parties may leave a review about an actual completed Reservation | Reviews, Search, and Ranking Rules |

## 5. Transparency Principle

Naudokis seeks to make the most important terms visible not only in legal documents, but also in the App at the moment when the User makes a decision: before submitting a Listing, before payment, before confirming a Reservation, during a dispute, and before leaving a review.
