This Privacy Policy explains how MB Naudokis ("Naudokis", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, discloses, and otherwise processes Personal Data.

The Policy applies when you use the Naudokis mobile App, related websites, administration interfaces, item-rental Platform services, payment, identity verification, support, Dispute resolution, and communication functions.

Naudokis is a mobile item-rental Platform operating in Lithuania. On the Platform, natural persons and business users may list items for rent, browse Listings, submit rental requests, authorize payments, hand over and return items, communicate with the other transaction party, leave reviews, and resolve rental-related matters through the Platform.

The Platform may support rental between private individuals (P2P), between businesses and private individuals (B2C), and between business entities (B2B), where permitted by Platform functions and applicable law.

**Scope of operations in Lithuania.** Naudokis operates in the Lithuanian market. Data related to tax, VAT, DAC7, VIES, Business User, payment, or payout compliance is processed only where such function is actually used, is required for Platform operation, or is required by legislation.

**Controller:** MB Naudokis, legal entity code 307423504, registered office address Numėjos g. 6, LT-08402 Vilnius, Lithuania. For privacy and support matters, you may contact us by email at **info@naudokis.lt**.

**Launch period** in this Policy means the period during which Naudokis operates in the Lithuanian market with the initial scope of functions, vendors, payments, payouts, categories, and technical processes described in this document. This is not a version with a lower level of privacy or security; the term only explains which functions and data Processing processes are currently actually enabled.

## How data is used in practice

These examples help explain when and why specific data is processed. They do not replace the detailed sections below.

- **When you create an account,** we process account, authentication, contact, legal-document acceptance, and security data so that we can provide access to the Platform and protect the account.
- **When you upload a Listing,** we process Listing information, photographs, category, price, location or pickup-area data, moderation status, and security signals so that the Listing can be displayed and reserved.
- **When you reserve an item,** we process Reservation dates, price, fees, Deposit, payment status, handover information, and communication with the other transaction party so that the rental can be concluded and performed.
- **When you verify identity,** we process verification status, selected verification results, and security signals. Naudokis retains raw document images only where and only to the extent necessary for a clearly stated purpose.
- **When a Dispute takes place,** we process messages, photographs, images, explanations, condition confirmations, payment information, and support-team decision records so that the claim can be assessed and the Deposit, refund, or another adjustment administered.
- **When you delete your account,** some data is deleted or anonymized, and some is retained on a limited basis where needed for active Reservations, Disputes, payments, taxes, accounting, security, or legal defence.

## 9. How data is shared with other Users

Naudokis is a two-sided rental Platform, so some data must be displayed or transferred to other Users in order to carry out Listings, Reservations, handover, return, payments, reviews, and Disputes. The amount of data depends on the Reservation stage, transaction type, User roles, and what is necessary for the specific function.

Main principles:

- before a confirmed Reservation, only limited public information needed for the Listing, trust, search, and Reservation decision is displayed;
- precise private contact, address, or handover information is disclosed only where necessary for a confirmed Reservation, handover, return, delivery, Dispute, security, taxes, B2B settlement, or legal purpose;
- during a Dispute, the substance of the claim, related evidence, and information necessary to respond may be disclosed to the other party, but Naudokis has the right to limit disclosure for reasons of privacy, security, fraud prevention, law enforcement, trade secrets, or third-party rights;
- for Business Users and Traders, more public business information may be displayed where required by consumer protection, the Digital Services Act, tax, invoicing, or other applicable law.

The following overview shows what other Users may see about you at different stages:

| Stage or situation | Data visible to other Users |
| --- | --- |
| Public Listing before Reservation | Listing information, photographs, price, Deposit, availability, approximate location, limited public Lessor identity, verification badge, ratings, reviews, and other public trust signals |
| Reservation request | Reservation dates, selected handover or delivery method, limited profile information of the parties, and information needed for the Lessor to accept or reject the request |
| After confirmed Reservation | Contact details, more precise pickup, delivery, or handover information, Reservation information, messages, and other data necessary to perform the rental |
| Handover and return | Handover and return confirmations, condition photographs, notes, accessories lists, timestamps, and other information necessary for performance |
