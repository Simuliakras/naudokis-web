## 15. Retention periods

**In brief:** We retain data only for as long as necessary for the Platform, law, taxes, accounting, security, Disputes, or lawful defence. Where possible, data is deleted, anonymized, or restricted.

We retain Personal Data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including service provision, account administration, payments, payouts, Deposits, accounting, taxes, DAC7 compliance, security, fraud prevention, Dispute handling, enforcement of Platform rules, legal requirements, and audit needs.

When the retention period expires or data is no longer needed, data is deleted, anonymized, or access to it is restricted, unless further retention is necessary for legal, accounting, tax, Dispute, payment, security, fraud-prevention, audit, or legal-defence reasons.

Current retention arrangement:

| Data category | Usual retention period or outcome |
| --- | --- |
| Account and profile data | While the account is active; after account deletion, deleted, anonymized, or retained on a limited basis if necessary for legal, accounting, payment, Dispute, or security purposes |
| Account deletion flow | 30-day grace period before final deletion if there are no active Reservations, Disputes, payments, or legal obstacles |
| Account deletion request records | Up to 180 days, retained on a limited basis only for deletion-request administration and audit purposes |
| Data export download links | 72 hours from generation |
| Chat content | Usually approximately 365 days; longer retention is possible for Disputes, security, support, legal requirements, or audit |

| Notification logs | Usually approximately 90 days |
| App notification inbox (in-app) | Usually approximately 90 days; deleted during account deletion unless limited retention is necessary for a Dispute or legal purpose |
| Telephone and email confirmation records | Usually approximately 30 days, unless necessary for security or account-compliance requirements |
| Moderation violations | Usually approximately 90 days; longer retention is possible because of repeated violations, security, Disputes, or legal reasons |
| Records of notices concerning Listings, Users, reviews, and other violations | Usually approximately 3 years where proportionate for security, Dispute, abuse-prevention, and legal requirement purposes |
| DSA notice, action, statement-of-reasons, and complaint records | Usually approximately 3 years or longer if necessary for a Dispute, authority request, repeated violations, security, or legal defence |

| Data independently processed by Stripe, Didit, or other regulated providers | Retained according to the relevant provider's privacy notice, legal obligations, and contracts; Naudokis retains only its required identifiers, statuses, and audit records |
| Payment, payout, Deposit, accounting, legal-document acceptance, tax, DAC7, self-billing, and legal-defence records | For statutory periods, which may reach 5-10 years or longer where required by legislation or where necessary for legal claims |
| Email delivery rejection and complaint list (suppression list) | A permanent rejection or complaint is retained for as long as necessary to avoid repeat sending to a non-functioning address or an address that submitted a complaint, with periodic necessity review; temporary rejection is usually retained for up to 14 days |
| Error diagnostics and Sentry or CloudWatch technical records | Usually for the shortest practically necessary period to diagnose errors and ensure security and service stability; access is restricted, and unnecessary Personal Data is removed or masked where possible |
| Amplitude analytics events | According to consent choices and for the period necessary for product analytics; non-essential analytics are disabled or not activated where there is no consent or it has been withdrawn |
| Inputs and outputs of OpenAI or other AI tools | Only to the extent necessary for the specific function, audit, security, Dispute, or quality purposes; must not be used for marketing profiling or a general User fraud score |
| Anonymized or aggregated data | May be retained for longer if it is no longer Personal Data and cannot reasonably be linked to a specific person |

Account deletion does not automatically eliminate data that we must retain or have a justified right to retain on a limited basis because of active Reservations, Disputes, chargebacks, accounting, taxes, DAC7, DSA, fraud prevention, Consumer rights, legal defence, or other mandatory obligations. In such cases, data is retained with limited access and used only for the relevant purpose.

## 16. Account deletion and data export

Naudokis provides the ability to delete an account independently and export data where this function is available in the App.

Account deletion may be blocked or postponed if there are active Reservations, unresolved Disputes, payments being processed, payouts, Deposits, chargebacks, legal holds, DSA or moderation complaints, tax, DAC7, accounting, or other unresolved Platform obligations.

When account deletion is placed in a queue, Naudokis disables ordinary login, starts a **30-day grace period**, sends a cancellation link by email, and after the grace period deletes, anonymizes, or restricts data if no obstacles remain.

Different data categories have different outcomes:

- profile, login data, device tokens, and notification settings are usually **deleted**;
- public Listings are usually **removed or anonymized**, unless it is necessary to temporarily retain information for an active Reservation, Dispute, legal claim, or security purpose;
- Reservations, reviews, and reputation records may be **anonymized or retained on a limited basis** in order to preserve operational, accounting, Dispute, and reputational integrity for the other transaction party;
- payment, payout, Deposit, tax, DAC7, DSA, legal-document acceptance, and legal-defence records are **retained on a limited basis** for statutory or reasonably necessary periods;
- Trader traceability data after the end of the relationship is usually retained for 6 months, unless longer retention is necessary under tax, accounting, payment, DAC7, Dispute, fraud-prevention, or other legal requirements.

After account deletion, your public profile information is removed to the extent possible, but some historical Reservation, payment, tax, Dispute, security, moderation, complaint, and legal records may be retained in limited form where required by legislation or where necessary for legal claims, fraud prevention, accounting, chargebacks, DSA obligations, or Platform security.

The self-service **data export** file is created in ZIP format and provided through a signed download link valid for **72 hours**. The link is sent to the verified User email address.

Frequency limits, security checks, and identity verification may apply to export requests. Personal Data exported in the ZIP archive is provided in JSON format: one file for each data category, together with `metadata.json`. This is a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable form under Article 20(1) GDPR.

The data export usually includes:

- profile information and identity verification status;
- Lessor statistics, your Listings, and your Reservations as Lessor or Renter;
- chat history, violation notices, and notification-delivery log metadata;
- favorite Listings, reviews, and recommendation information;
- Stripe settlement identifiers, tax profile, payment history, and Deposit statuses;
- mute and block records and legal-document acceptance history.

The data export usually does not include tax-compliance documents prepared by Naudokis as a reporting entity, for example final DAC7 XML/PDF artifacts, where their disclosure could violate the rights of other persons, tax administration interests, or restrictions provided by legislation.

The export may also exclude internal security, fraud-prevention, moderation, trade-secret, other User, or third-party data where disclosure would violate the rights of other persons, Platform security, or restrictions provided by legislation.

If mandatory reporting applies to a specific Lessor, we may provide related report information to that Lessor through the App or support channel to the extent provided by applicable law and Platform functions.

### 22.1 Appendix A: summary of retention periods and deletion rules

Naudokis retains Personal Data only for as long as necessary to achieve the specific purpose, comply with legal requirements, resolve Disputes, prevent fraud, perform accounting, taxes, payment reconciliation, Platform security, and protect legitimate interests. When data is no longer needed, it is deleted, anonymized, or restricted so that it is no longer used for active Platform functions, except for lawful retention.

| Data group | Typical retention principle | What may block immediate deletion |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account and profile data | While the account is active, later for a limited period for security, Dispute, and legal-claim purposes | Active Reservations, Disputes, debts, payment Disputes, investigations, tax or accounting obligations |
| Reservations, payments, Deposits, refunds, and payouts | According to accounting, tax, payment-reconciliation, Dispute, and legal requirements | DAC7, VAT, i.SAF, Stripe or payment Disputes, court or authority proceedings |
| Identity verification statuses, audit records, and document technical security tag | While needed for trust, security, fraud prevention, payments, payouts, or legal requirements; verification may be considered valid for up to 730 days if there is no earlier expiry or need for re-check; the document technical security tag is retained as part of the identity verification record and is usually deleted upon final account deletion, unless an active investigation, Dispute, payment Dispute, legal obligation, or legal defence applies | Fraud risk, prevention of multiple accounts, bypassing account blocks or other restrictions, Stripe or KYC requirements, Disputes, law-enforcement or payment investigations |
| Trader and DSA traceability data | Retained while necessary for the Platform relationship and legal requirements; DSA traceability data is usually retained for 6 months after the end of the relationship, unless another lawful basis requires longer retention | Taxes, DAC7, accounting, Consumer Disputes, law-enforcement or authority requests |
| Messages, support, Disputes, and evidence | While needed for the Reservation, support, Disputes, security, and legal requirements | Active Dispute, damage claim, law enforcement, Consumer authorities, payment Dispute |
| Analytics, diagnostics, and security logs | As short as possible according to security, service operation, error correction, and audit needs | Security incidents, abuse investigations, technical reasons |
| Marketing consents and notification choices | While the consent or choice is valid and for a limited period for evidence purposes | Proof of consent, proof of opt-out, delivery rejection or complaint lists |

An account deletion request usually means that Naudokis suspends active account use and deletes or anonymizes data that is no longer needed. Some data cannot be deleted immediately if it is necessary to perform a contract, complete a Reservation, refund a payment, administer a Deposit, resolve a Dispute, comply with tax, accounting, or DAC7 obligations, defend legal claims, or ensure Platform security.
