# Naudokis Prohibited and Restricted Items Rules

Effective date: June 1, 2026
Version: 2026-06-01
Language: English
Last updated: June 1, 2026
Document ID: **NAUDOKIS-RESTRICTED-ITEMS-EN-2026-06-01**

These Rules form part of the Naudokis Terms of Use and must be read together with the main document. If a special term is provided in the App for a specific Reservation, Listing, User role, or Platform function, it applies to the extent it does not conflict with mandatory law.

## Product safety and traceability standard

The Lessor must ensure that the item is safe to use for its intended purpose, has not been recalled from the market, is not stolen, counterfeit, illegal, or misleadingly described.

If usage instructions, safety warnings, age restrictions, protective equipment, manufacturer recommendations, maintenance requirements, or qualification requirements apply to the item, the Lessor must clearly state this in the Listing and pass the information to the Renter.

Naudokis may request additional information about the item's origin, condition, serial number, maintenance, instructions, manufacturer warnings, insurance requirements, or permit requirements. If the information is not provided or a justified safety risk arises, the Listing may be hidden, suspended, or removed.

Users may report dangerous, recalled, illegal, or misleading items through the App or by email at **info@naudokis.lt**.

## 7. Listings, prohibited items, and higher-risk categories

**In brief:** The Lessor must list only lawful, safe, and accurately described items. During the launch period, some categories may be restricted, hidden, or allowed only after administrator review.

**This section is most important for Lessors and Business Lessors.** It explains which items may be listed, what information must be provided, and when a Listing may be rejected or suspended.

### Listings

Lessors may create Listings only for items that may be lawfully rented. Listings may be drafts, pending review, active, paused, suspended, or deleted. Only active Listings are publicly visible.

A Listing must be accurate, complete, clear, and not misleading. It must provide the information the Renter needs in order to make a decision, including:

- title, description, category, and condition;
- photographs, price, Deposit if applicable, and availability;
- rental duration limits, cancellation policy, and pickup or delivery options;
- usage restrictions, defects, safety instructions, and other material information.

The Lessor is responsible for the accuracy of the Listing and for ensuring that the item matches the description provided in the Listing and is lawful, safe, clean, functioning, and suitable for the stated use.

The stated replacement value must be justified and correspond to the real value of the item. An unjustifiably high replacement value may not serve as a basis for requiring a higher Deposit or refusing to refund a Deposit.

Naudokis has the right to review, reject, edit, pause, suspend, hide, or remove Listings that violate these Terms, Platform rules, legislation, prohibited-item requirements, safety standards, consumer protection rules, payment-provider requirements, or other compliance requirements.

Unless the App states otherwise, confirmed Reservations are usually available for a period of 1-60 days and are paid in euros.

Some Listing rental-duration fields or preliminary price estimates may allow a longer period to be entered for preparation, administration, or future functions. However, during the launch period, the 60-day limit applies to the Reservation submission and confirmation process, unless the App clearly states otherwise.

During the launch period, Listings must usually relate to items located in Lithuania, Lithuanian handover or delivery locations, and Lithuanian settlement and payout rules supported by the Platform. The terms, price, Deposit, delivery fees, applicable restrictions, and availability of the specific Reservation are shown in the App.

### Prohibited items

You may not list, offer, rent, or attempt to rent items that are illegal, unsafe, stolen, counterfeit, rights-infringing, misleading, not actually possessed by the Lessor or impossible for the Lessor to hand over, unsuitable for the stated use, recalled from the market, non-compliant with product safety requirements, or prohibited under these Terms, Platform rules, or applicable law.

Prohibited or restricted items include, among others:

- weapons, ammunition, explosives, pyrotechnics, electric-shock devices, firearm parts, cold weapons (including prohibited knives), and other objects intended for violence or injury;
- controlled substances, narcotic or psychotropic substances, dangerous chemicals, toxic, radioactive, or biohazardous materials;
- stolen, counterfeit, smuggled, internationally sanctioned, or intellectual-property-infringing items;
- items for which offering, transfer, or use requires a licence, permit, registration, special qualification, special protection, mandatory insurance, or age verification, if the Lessor cannot document that these requirements are met or the App does not expressly permit that category;
- vehicles, trailers, motorized or electric devices, drones, watercraft or aircraft subject to registration, insurance, traffic, permit, or special safety regime requirements, unless the specific category is clearly allowed in the App and all additional rules are complied with;
- items intended for children or infants, medical, health, rehabilitation, or hygiene items that do not meet safety, hygiene, instruction, disinfection, or mandatory labelling requirements, or whose rental may create a disproportionate health risk;
- batteries, accumulators, chargers, power tools, heating, gas, pressure, cutting, lifting, climbing, or construction equipment if damaged, improperly maintained, without instructions, without required protective equipment, or plainly unsuitable for safe consumer use;
- tickets, financial instruments, payment instruments, identity documents, access cards, software licences, databases, or other intangible or person-specific objects (for example, named rights or access rights), unless the App clearly permits the specific category;
- medicines, food, supplements, cosmetics, alcohol, tobacco and related products, and other consumable products, which are prohibited in all cases; animals, unless the specific category is clearly permitted in the App and is lawful;
- sexual goods, intimate-use or direct-body-contact personal hygiene goods that cannot be reliably disinfected, items containing Personal Data, or items whose transfer could violate privacy, dignity, or security requirements;
- other items that Naudokis reasonably recognizes as incompatible with the Platform's purpose, safety, payment-provider rules, insurance or product safety requirements, or legislation.

### Higher-risk categories during the launch period

During the launch period, higher-risk categories are not considered automatically publicly permitted merely because they exist in the Platform category list, search, database, or administration configuration. Such categories may be hidden, paused, allowed only for administrator-approved Listings, or allowed only with additional safety conditions.

Higher-risk categories include, among others:

- drones;
- vehicles, trailers, motorcycles, electric vehicles, and similar mobility devices;
- medical, rehabilitation, hygiene, and mobility equipment;
- child and infant safety equipment;
- batteries, accumulators, chargers, and electrical devices;
- electrical, cutting, lifting, climbing, construction, heating, gas, or pressure equipment;
- telephones or other devices that may contain Personal Data;
- other categories to which product safety, licensing, insurance, registration, hygiene, age, instruction, defect disclosure, or special-permit requirements may apply.

For higher-risk categories, Naudokis has the right to require one or more additional conditions, for example:

- administrator review before publication;
- mandatory instructions, defect disclosure, and photographs of condition and accessories;
- handover and return confirmations;
- proof of mandatory insurance or a permit held by the Lessor;
- age check or identity verification;
- special usage restrictions;
- higher or Platform-set Deposit;
- additional User confirmation;
- payout hold until return;
- other proportionate safety and compliance measures.

If a Listing is created in an incorrect category, incorrectly bypasses a category restriction, or it later becomes clear that the item should have been assigned to a prohibited or higher-risk category, Naudokis has the right to reclassify, hide, suspend, or remove it, cancel related Reservations, hold payouts, or take other proportionate measures under these Terms and applicable law.

### Category visibility, restricted categories, and administrator approval

Platform categories and subcategories may have different visibility and permission statuses. The fact that a category exists in the internal Platform configuration does not in itself mean that all Users may publicly list items in it.

Naudokis has the right to apply at least the following category visibility statuses:

- **public** - a category in which a Listing may be created and published if the ordinary requirements of these Terms, safety, payments, confirmation, and moderation are met;
- **list-only or internal** - a category may be shown in lists or used for internal Platform operation, but its public use to create a Listing may be limited;
- **administrator** - a category that may require separate permission or administrator review before public publication of the Listing;
- **deferred or preparation** - a category prepared for internal preparation but not yet activated for public use;
- **prohibited or disabled** - an item type that may not be publicly listed, reserved, or transferred through the Platform; such prohibition usually applies not through a separate category visibility status, but through a separately maintained prohibited and restricted items list (see below and Appendix 23.6).

Naudokis has the right to maintain the prohibited and restricted items list through Platform administration tools. Such list may be updated in order to ensure compliance with legislation, product safety, payment-provider, insurance, consumer protection, or Platform trust and safety requirements.

Users have no right to rely on the fact that a specific prohibited or restricted item was not expressly mentioned in the examples if, by its nature, it is illegal, unsafe, misleading, rights-infringing, or incompatible with the Platform's purpose.

### 23.6 Prohibited and restricted items appendix

This appendix supplements Section 7 and helps explain which items may not be listed or may be listed only after additional Naudokis approval. The fact that an item is not expressly mentioned in the list does not mean that it may be listed if, by its nature, it is illegal, unsafe, misleading, rights-infringing, regulated, unsuitable for a Consumer, or incompatible with the Platform's purpose.

During the launch period, Naudokis has the right to prohibit, restrict, or route for administrator review the following categories or items:

- vehicles, trailers, motorcycles, scooters, drones, watercraft, or aircraft;
- medical, health, rehabilitation, hygiene, or infant or child safety items;
- batteries, accumulators, chargers, and higher-risk electric tools;
- cutting, heating, gas, pressure, lifting, climbing, construction, or industrial equipment;
- items requiring a licence, permit, registration, insurance, special training, protective equipment, or age verification;
- expensive electronics or professional-equipment items without proper identification, condition recording, or handover evidence.

Naudokis has the right to require additional fields, instructions, defect disclosure, condition photographs, an accessories list, serial number, confirmation of ownership rights, proof of insurance or permit, age or identity check, higher Deposit, administrator approval, or other conditions.

If an item is incorrectly assigned to a lower-risk category, bypasses a restriction, or it later becomes clear that additional requirements apply to it, Naudokis has the right to reclassify, hide, suspend, or remove it, cancel related Reservations, hold payouts, or take other proportionate measures.

Product safety notices may also be handled under Section 19 of the Terms of Use ("Liability, insurance, and product safety"). Where a notice relates to an item that is potentially unsafe, recalled, illegal, or dangerous to Consumers, Naudokis has the right to inform affected Users, suspend related Reservations, and cooperate with market surveillance authorities to the extent required or permitted by applicable law.

The Lessor remains responsible for ensuring that the item is lawful, safe, properly maintained, clean, supplied with necessary accessories, instructions, warnings, and, where necessary, protective equipment. Naudokis category or Listing approval does not mean that Naudokis guarantees the item's safety, quality, suitability, or compliance with all legislation.
