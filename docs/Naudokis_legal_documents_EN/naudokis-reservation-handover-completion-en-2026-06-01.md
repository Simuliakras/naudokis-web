# Naudokis Reservation, Handover, and Completion Process Rules

Effective date: June 1, 2026
Version: 2026-06-01
Document ID: **NAUDOKIS-RENTAL-JOURNEY-EN-2026-06-01**

These Rules explain the practical Naudokis Reservation flow. They help clarify when a Reservation is considered confirmed, when a payment is considered completed, how handover is recorded, and when a rental is considered completed.

## 1. Reservation statuses

| Status | What it means | Practical consequence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Request created | The Renter selected the item and dates and submitted a request | The Lessor must still accept or reject it |
| Payment being authorized | Stripe checks the payment instrument and the amounts applicable to the Reservation | Funds may be reserved or prepared for capture according to the Stripe process |
| Accepted by Lessor | The Lessor agreed to the request | Payment may be captured or finally confirmed |
| Confirmed and paid | Payment has been successfully completed through the Platform | The parties must perform the Reservation according to the terms |
| In progress | The item has been handed over or the rental period has started | The Renter is responsible for careful use and return |
| Being returned | The rental period is ending or the item is being handed back | The parties must check condition and accessories |
| Completed | The item has been returned and there is no active claim | The Deposit refund and payout process is initiated, if applicable |
| Disputed | A claim has been submitted concerning damage, loss, delay, cancellation, or payment | The Deposit, refund, or payout may be temporarily held |
| Cancelled | The Reservation has been terminated under the cancellation rules | Refund, fee, and no-show consequences apply |

## 2. Before the Lessor accepts the request

Until the Lessor accepts, the Reservation is not yet finally confirmed. Naudokis may check:

- Listing availability;
- User verification status;
- payment instrument readiness;
- Deposit and price amounts;
- risk relating to prohibited or restricted items;
- account, payment, compliance, or security restrictions.

If the request is rejected, expires, or cannot be confirmed for payment, identity, Listing, or security reasons, the refund or cancellation of the payment authorization is carried out according to the Payments Rules and the Stripe process.

## 3. Handover standard

When handing over an item, it is recommended and, where required by the App, mandatory to:

- confirm the handover time;
- check the identity of the item and accessories;
- take photographs of the item from several angles;
- photograph existing defects, scratches, wear, serial numbers, or included components;
- hand over usage instructions, safety warnings, chargers, cases, keys, or other accessories;
- clearly mark in the App or in messages if something does not match the Listing.

The Renter must immediately report if, at the time of receipt, the item does not match the Listing, is not functioning, is unsafe, is dirty, has missing accessories, or shows undescribed damage.

## 4. Use period

The Renter must use the item only normally, lawfully, safely, and according to the purpose stated in the Listing or instructions. The Renter may not:

- transfer the item to another person without permission;
- sublease, sell, pledge, or modify the item;
- use the item dangerously, unlawfully, or for a commercial purpose if this was not agreed;
- hide, leave, or keep the item in unreasonably risky conditions;
- remove identification marks, accessories, or safety elements.

## 5. Return standard

When returning the item, it is necessary to:

- return it at the agreed time and agreed place;
- return all accessories;
- return it in the condition in which it was received, taking into account ordinary wear typical of rental;
- report any incident, fault, loss, theft, or delay;
- cooperate in taking return photographs and confirming the return time.

## 6. When a Reservation is considered completed

A Reservation is considered completed for purposes of the Platform process when:

- the rental period has ended;
- the item has been returned or is reasonably considered returned on the Platform;
- there is no active substantiated claim concerning damage, loss, delay, non-conformity, or a payment Dispute; and
- the Platform can initiate the Deposit refund or payout process under the Payments and Deposit Rules.

Completion of a Reservation on the Platform does not eliminate a party's right to bring a lawful claim if circumstances emerge after completion that could not reasonably have been identified at the time of return, but such claims are assessed according to evidence, time limits, and applicable law.

## 7. Why it is important to act through the Platform

Payments, messages, handover confirmations, photographs, and Dispute information submitted through the Platform help Naudokis fairly assess the course of events. If the parties agree outside the Platform, Naudokis may not have sufficient evidence to help resolve a Dispute, administer a Deposit, or protect the other party.
